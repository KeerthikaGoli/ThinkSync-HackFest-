Rules & Eligibility

✅ Eligibility

  • 📚Open to all students, professionals, freelancers, and innovators from any background
  • 🎂Must be 16+ years old. Under 18 requires parental/guardian consent
  • 👥Team size: 1-4 members. Solo participation allowed
  • 🏫Teams can include members from different colleges/organizations
  • 🎯No restriction on academic stream or specialization
  • ⚠️Each participant can be part of only ONE team

📋 Key Rules

  • 🚀Original Work: Projects must be developed during hackathon only
  • ❌No Plagiarism: Pre-built projects will lead to disqualification
  • 🔧Open-source allowed: Public APIs and frameworks with proper credit
  • 📤Submit: Source code, documentation, demo video (2-4 min), working prototype
  • 🤖AI Tools: Allowed with full disclosure (ChatGPT, Copilot, etc.)
  • ⚖️IP Rights: Project ownership remains with team

⚠️ Disqualification Grounds

• Copied or pre-developed work • Misconduct or harassment • Missing submission guidelines/deadlines • Manipulating judging results
